About Em nuel

Em nuel is a seasoned criminal defense attorney and legal analyst with over 15 years of experience representing clients in complex criminal cases, personal injury claims, and constitutional law matters. As a leading contributor to Legal Maths, Em nuel brings real-world courtroom experience to every article, helping readers understand the practical implications of legal decisions.

After graduating with honors from the University of Michigan Law School, Em nuel served as a public defender before founding a successful criminal defense practice. This unique perspective—seeing the justice system from both sides—informs every piece of content produced for Legal Maths.

Em nuel has been featured in numerous legal publications and has spoken at national conferences on criminal defense strategies, digital evidence, and the intersection of technology and the law.
